Sea Freight

As one of the quality sea freight service providers in China, we offer a full range of ocean freight services for all your ocean freight needs.

China Freight's reputation comes from our ability to precisely coordinate between numerous vessels, container ships, and bulk ships in international ocean freights to ensure meeting every single one of your shipping needs. From appointing the right vessel for your requirements up to handling and preparing the necessary paperwork, China Freight manages every part of the shipping process in order to establish a timely arrival of your cargo.

Our esteemed partnerships with carriers and the comprehensive network of highly distinguished agents enable us to deliver prompt shipping options that consist of port to port, door to door deliveries, shipping to Amazon FBA, and more. We are able to provide prompt deliveries of any size of cargo and greatly reduce the estimated time of arrival through our considerably large network with ocean carriers. Our years of experience enable us to uphold international standards in freight forwarding solutions and provide you first rate services at largely competitive rates.

Services Include:

  • Full Container Load (FCL)
  • Less Than Container Load (LCL)
  • BreakBulk and Roll On/Roll Off
  • Door to Door, Door to Port, Port to Port, Port to Door Services
  • Shipping to Amazon FBA
  • Freight Consolidation
  • Warehousing and distribution
  • Regular & Reliable Sailing Schedules
  • Inland Trucking
  • Import & Export Services
  • Document Preparation
  • Customs clearance
